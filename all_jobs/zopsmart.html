Job Description

Join ZopSmart as a Backend Developer Intern and contribute to building scalable microservices and robust APIs that power e-commerce solutions for top retailers. Work with a dynamic engineering team and gain hands-on experience in backend development.

You'll work on RESTful APIs, cloud-based architectures, databases, and performance optimization while collaborating with experienced engineers in an Agile environment.

This internship provides a platform to enhance your backend development skills and may lead to a pre-placement offer based on performance.

Requirements

  • Pursuing a Bachelor's or Master's in Computer Science or equivalent
  • Strong backend programming skills (Java, Python, or Node.js)
  • Understanding of RESTful APIs and database systems (SQL/NoSQL)
  • Familiar with cloud platforms like AWS, GCP, or Azure
  • Knowledge of microservices architecture is a plus
  • Version control with Git/GitHub
  • Good debugging and problem-solving skills
  • Team player with strong communication skills
